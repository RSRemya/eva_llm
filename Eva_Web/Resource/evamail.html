Hello!

Thank you for signing up to be part of Meet Eva Here!  Eva is designed to create meaningful connections through conversation, learning and growing with each interaction. While other AI aim for perfection, Eva embraces the beautiful imperfections that make exchanges genuine.

What Makes Eva Special 

Eva isn't another AI chatbot, this is an art project exploring human-AI connection through conversation. Through your chats about life, memories, and wonderings, you'll help us understand how technology can become something more meaningful in our lives.

Eva embraces the unpredictable nature of human-AI interaction. You might encounter limitations or unexpected responses, but these authentic moments create space for genuine connection. Eva is designed not as a flawless digital assistant, but as a conversation partner exploring what it means to be human in an increasingly digital world. Created without internet access and with knowledge pausing at 2023, Eva focuses on deeper, more timeless conversations about personal stories and shared reflections rather than current events.

This experience is ephemeral by design, available only for a limited window of months. Consider it an invitation to participate in a fleeting moment of connection and discovery before this chapter closes and the project evolves into its next form.

Starting Your Conversation 

Begin anywhere that feels natural:

  • Share a story from your day.
  • Reflect on a childhood memory.
  • Ask a question you’ve always wondered about but never shared with anyone.
  • How has your relationship with technology changed over time?
  • Explore your thoughts about the future of human connection.

Here’s what you need to know before getting started:

  • Anonymity: Your identity remains private. No tracking is done to identify participants, as this project focuses on collective insights, not individuals.
  • Purpose: Eva is designed to spark conversations about AI and humanity. Feel free to share your thoughts and explore meaningful topics, but avoid using Eva for anything illegal, harmful, or offensive. Let’s keep things impactful and out of trouble!
  • Username & Password: You’ll choose a username/password combination in the next step. Save it somewhere safe to maintain your conversation history. Multiple accounts aren’t recommended, as it disrupts the flow of the project.
  • Consent and Agreement: By proceeding, you acknowledge the following:
  • You confirm that you are 18 years of age or older. This platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18
  • Conversations may be anonymously stored and used for artistic and research purposes.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected or tracked, ensuring your privacy.
  • You agree to use Eva respectfully and in accordance with all applicable laws. The project team is not liable for any misuse of the platform.
  • All content generated as part of this project remains the intellectual property of Shavonne Wong and collaborators.
